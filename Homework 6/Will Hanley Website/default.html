Restaurants

It can be challenging to be a vegetarian when you go out to eat, and it is only getting worse in Missoula. As the town has grown and changed, so many of our favorite places are gone. Though the assignment was to make a list of our favorite restuarants, I think a list of my favorite restaurants that have closed is more interesting.

  • Food for Thought - Good sandwiches and lots of options.
  • Hob Nob - Great migas and sweet potato fries
  • The 'old' Old Post - Great mushroom burger and fried goat cheese salad.
